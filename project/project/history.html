Seattle Symphony Basketball Team

HISTORY IN THE MAKING

Our Banners in the Rafters:

2021 Western Conference Champions

The Seattle Symphony won the 2021 Western Conference Finals championship against the Sacramento Gold Rush in a 4-1 series victory. Rob Bequette won the Western Conference Finals

2022 League Champions

The Seattle Symphony won the 2022 league championship against the Miami Cyclones in a 4-0 series sweep. Michael Shepherd won the Finals MVP award.

A Brief History:

The Seattle Symphony was added in 2020 into the NABL (North American Basketball League), which covers 3 nations (USA, Canada, and Mexico). The team has reached the NABL finals 2X and won the league championship 1X. The team is the first team in NABL history to reach the finals within the 2 seasons of formation.
