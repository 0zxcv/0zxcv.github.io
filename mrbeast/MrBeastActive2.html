Mr Beast

ACTIVITY IN COMMUNITY

Although Mr Beast usually films within and gives his to own community, perhaps the biggest project Mr Beast has worked on is "Team Trees" which he created a website where people could donate money. Every dollar donated equals one tree planted. The end goal was $20,000,000 to plant 20,000,000 trees. After large donations from businesses and celebrities like Elon Musk, and countless other donations by millions of other people globally, that goal of $20,000,000 was soon surpassed.
